About Vanqueer Building

Experience urban living redefined at Vanqueer, where every moment is crafted to perfection.

Our Services

Vanqeuur: A Haven of Elevated Services
Vanqeuur goes beyond stunning apartments and breathtaking views. It offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to pamper your every need and streamline your life, allowing you to focus on what truly matters. Here's a glimpse into the world of unparalleled service that awaits you

  • Rooftop Oasis: Relax and recharge at the infinity pool, soak up the sun on the landscaped sundeck, or unwind amidst the serenity of the rooftop garden.
  • State-of-the-Art Fitness Center: Maintain your wellness routine in a high-tech gym equipped with cutting-edge equipment and fitness classes.
  • In-House Entertainment: Host movie nights in the luxurious in-house theater, or gather with friends in the vibrant game room featuring billiards, arcade games, and more.
  • 24/7 Concierge Service: A dedicated concierge team is available around the clock to assist with anything from restaurant reservations and travel arrangements to package delivery and dry-cleaning pickup.
  • In-Residence Services: Schedule housekeeping, pet care, or personal chef services directly through Vanqeuur's resident portal, ensuring your home is always cared for.
  • Business Center: Stay productive without leaving the building. The business center offers private workstations, soundproofed conference rooms, and high-speed Wi-Fi.
  • On-Site Valet Parking: Eliminate the stress of parking with valet service that takes care of your car upon arrival and return.
  • Welcome Package: Upon arrival, be greeted with a personalized welcome package featuring gourmet treats, luxurious bath amenities, and city guides.
  • Priority Access: Enjoy exclusive resident discounts at nearby restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues.
  • Pet-Friendly Amenities: Vanqeuur welcomes your furry companions with a dedicated dog park, pet grooming services (upon request), and a network of dog walker recommendations.
  • Security and Peace of Mind: Rest assured with 24/7 security personnel, secure building access systems, and in-unit safes.
